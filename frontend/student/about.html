About Us

Hostel-EZ is a cutting-edge digital ecosystem designed to revolutionize everyday hostel operations. We've replaced dusty registers and manual chaos with a centralized platform for complaints, attendance, and transparency.

Our Mission

To digitalize hostel paperwork, ensuring maximum safety, real-time tracking, and 100% transparency between students and administration.

Dual-Interface

Tailored experiences for both Students and Admins. Whether you're booking a late-night snack or managing floor attendance, it's all one click away.

Cloud-Backed

All data is securely stored and synced via Google Sheets and Apps Script — no heavy servers, no downtime, accessible from any device.
